Apple Calendar (iPad or iPhone)

  1. Copy the URL listed above
  2. Open the "Settings" app on your mobile device
  3. Look in the left-hand pane
  4. Tap "Mail, Contacts, Calendars"
  5. Look in the right-hand pane
  6. Tap "Add Account..."
  7. Tap "Other"
  8. Tap "Add Subscribed Calendar"
  9. Paste the URL into the "Server" box
  10. Tap the "Next" button
  11. Tap the "Save" button

Now you can view your appointments in your Calendar

Apple iCal (Desktop or Laptop)

  1. Copy the URL listed above
  2. Open iCal
  3. Look at the top of your screen
  4. Click "Calendar"
  5. Click "Subscribe..."
  6. Paste the URL in the "Calendar URL" box
  7. Click "Subscribe"
  8. Click "Add Subscribed Calendar"
  9. Paste the URL into the "Server" box
  10. Click the "Next" button
  11. Click the "Save" button

Now you can view your appointments in your Calendar

Google Calendar (Mobile Device)

  1. Copy the URL listed above
  2. Open Google Calendar
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the screen
  4. Tap on the "View: Desktop" link
  5. Scroll up and zoom into the left-hand pane
  6. Tap the arrow to the right of "Other calendars"
  7. Select "Add by URL"
  8. Paste the URL into the "URL" box
  9. Tap "Add Calendar"

Now you can view your appointments in your Calendar

Google Calendar (Desktop or Laptop)

  1. Copy the URL listed above
  2. Open Google Calendar
  3. Look in the left-hand pane
  4. Click the arrow to the right of "Other calendars"
  5. Select "Add by URL"
  6. Paste the URL into the "URL" box
  7. Click "Add Calendar"
